Quick Guide

Deleting Objects

Double-click (desktop) or long-press (mobile) on any object or line to delete it. No confirmation needed — use undo to recover.

Animation Playback

Click Play to watch lines animate in order. Each line type animates differently: skating shows a player moving, puck carry shows player + trailing puck, passing and shooting show the puck flying from a stationary player. Use Step to advance one line at a time, Loop for continuous replay, and the speed selector (1/3, 2/3, Full) to control pace.

Fullscreen Mode

Click Full in the toolbar to expand the rink to 95% of the screen, hiding all other UI. Press ESC or click Exit Fullscreen to return.

Tags & Categories

Below the drill description, type a tag name and press Enter. Tags double as categories in the Library. A drill tagged "Skating" and "Warmup" appears in both category sections. Remove a tag by clicking its × button. On mobile, tags are hidden to save space — switch to a wider screen to manage them.

Share via Link

Click Share to generate a URL + QR code for the current drill. Send the link or scan the QR — the recipient gets the drill auto-imported into their library. You can also share an entire practice plan from the Practice Plan tab.

Practice Plans & Timer

The Practice Plan tab lets you build sessions from your library drills. Add breaks ("Water Break"), set durations, reorder, and export as PDF. You can save multiple plans and assign them to days in the weekly calendar. Click Start Timer to run the plan live with a full-screen countdown that auto-advances through each entry.

Print Cards

In the Library tab, click Print Cards to generate a PDF with drill cards — choose 1, 2, or 4 per page. Each card has a rink thumbnail, title, tags, and description. Enter a team name for a header on each page.

Theme & Display

Click the Theme button in the footer to cycle between System, Light, and Dark modes. Your choice is saved. On small screens, the UI automatically compacts to give the rink more space.

Download Confirmations

All actions that download files (Save JSON, PDF, Export Library, Print Cards) require a double-tap to confirm — first tap shows "Confirm?", second tap executes. Auto-cancels after 3 seconds.

Keyboard Shortcuts

ESC — deselect tool / exit fullscreen  |  Ctrl/Cmd+Z — undo  |  Ctrl/Cmd+Y — redo

Data & Storage

Everything is saved in your browser's local storage — no accounts, no cloud. Use Export Library to back up all drills as one JSON file. Use Import Library to restore (merge or replace).
